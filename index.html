Biography

Currently, I am a PhD student in Spatial Statistics based at the University of Cincinnati, USA. I am also TA in spatial statistics at the Geography Department.

Previously, I worked at the the Colombian Center for Computational Biology (CO), LiveVox (CA), and CREATIS-UMR at the Université Lyon 1 (FR).

My day-to-day research covers applying statistical methods to model components of population change and infectious diseases. This website contains posts mainly related to my research using R, side jobs in image processing and scientific applications, as well as some information on my publications. It is a successor to my google sites.
